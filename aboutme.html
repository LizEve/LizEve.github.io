Project - Is the trill gone? How repeated loss of a sexually selected trait impacts the evolution of a speciose anuran family

I am investigating the loss of male advertisement calls and related morphological structures in True Toads (Bufonidae). Little is known about how the loss of sexually selected traits affects the evoluiton and diversification of a lineage. Vocal sacs in anurans (frogs and toads) are an ideal trait and study system to examine the loss of a sexually selected trait, because of the direct connection between morphology and advertisement calls, which are used to attract mates and guard territory.
