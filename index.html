About Me

I am an NSF Postdoctoral Fellow based out of Utah State University with a joint appointment at University of California Berkeley.

My research spans phylogenetics, methods development, comparative evolution, and conservation genetics all with a focus on herpetofauna. I am generally interested in quantifying and synthesizing variation in large phylogenetic datasets to obtain information that is otherwise overlooked.

I also really like toads.
